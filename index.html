01. From Big 4 Discipline to Big Data Analysis

My career began in the deep end of traditional finance. Training as a Chartered Accountant at EY, I managed large-scale assurance engagements for FTSE 100 banks. That experience taught me the importance of rigor, accuracy, and how to navigate the pressure of a deadline.


But I also noticed a problem: Finance teams were spending too much time gathering data and not enough time analyzing it.


The Pivot: I decided to stop fighting spreadsheets and start engineering solutions. I taught myself Python and SQL, realizing that the repetitive tasks slowing us down could be automated. Today, I operate at the intersection of Finance and Tech. I don't just build budgets; I build the systems that make budgeting easier.


Here are a few technologies I've been working with recently:

  • Python (Pandas)
  • Datarails
  • SQL / BigQuery
  • PowerBI
  • Advanced Excel
  • Alteryx
